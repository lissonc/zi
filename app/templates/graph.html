About the Graph

This visualization shows how characters and primitives relate to each other. The arrows indicate that a primitive is used to form a character.

You can:

  • Click and drag to move the graph
  • Scroll to zoom in and out
  • Click on any node to go to its detail page
  • Use the control buttons to adjust the view

This graph helps understand the building blocks of characters and how they're structured in Heisig's system.
